Training for Meaningful Conversation

Want to feel more confident speaking with people who disagree with you? Here are some tips and exercises to build that skill.

Tips for Constructive Dialogue

  • 🎧 Listen to understand, not to win
  • 💬 Ask open-ended questions
  • 🧘‍♀️ Pause before reacting
  • 🙋‍♂️ Share from your own experience ("I" statements)
  • 📌 Stay curious, not combative

Practice Exercise

Pair up with someone and roleplay a disagreement on a workplace issue (e.g., working from home, union strategy). Take turns being the speaker and the listener. Use the tips above!
